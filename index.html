Are payment plans available?
Yes! We offer flexible payment options to make things easier:
Fees under $1,500: payable in full at enrolment.

Fees over $1,500: a deposit of $1,200 is required, with the remaining balance to be finalised within 6 months or upon completion of your qualification, whichever comes first.
①
Can I complete single units only, or must I complete a full Skill Set or Qualification?
You can absolutely complete individual units. You don't need to commit to a full qualification or Skill Set.
Certificate IV units: $350 per unit

Diploma units: $400 per unit
📅
How long is the enrolment period?
Your enrolment lasts 12 months. We recommend aiming to complete roughly one unit per month to stay on track.
Need more time? If you're making steady progress, we offer a free extension, no stress, no extra fees.
🚀
How do I get started?
It's simple. Just three steps:

1. Email or call us to make an enquiry
2. Complete a short pre-enrolment questionnaire
3. Finalise your full enrolment

We'll guide you through every step. No confusing forms or hoops to jump through.
🎓
Will I have the same trainer throughout?
Yes, absolutely. You'll be assigned a dedicated trainer who stays with you for your entire qualification. Consistency matters, and we want you to build a strong working relationship with your trainer.
If for any reason you'd like to switch trainers, just let us know and we'll arrange it. You're in control.
👥
Do I need others to help me facilitate the practical components of my training?
Yes, you will. You'll need access to others to help support your learning journey. The specific requirements are outlined in the qualification details on our website, and we will also guide and support you throughout your enrolment.
You're never left to figure this out alone. We walk you through exactly what you need and help you make it work.
💻
I feel unsure about my digital literacy. Can I still study online with HBTA?
Absolutely. Rest assured we support you every step of the way. Our structure is one of mentoring and guidance, and we will help you develop your digital literacy as you work through any course you undertake with us.
We guide you so that you never feel overwhelmed. Building your confidence online is part of the journey with us.
🌟
Do you offer non-accredited training to support Professional Development?
Absolutely! We tailor our Professional Development to suit each organisation's goals and outcomes. Our approach is highly effective because we take the time to fully understand what your organisation needs to achieve before we design a single thing.
No generic, off-the-shelf content. Every PD program is built around your people and your goals.
★
Is RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) available?
Yes! We work closely with you to discuss your evidence and experience. Together we'll determine if a blended approach makes sense, combining RPL with targeted training to fill any gaps.
We keep it practical and personalised. No cookie-cutter process. We look at your experience and tailor the pathway.
⇄
Can I get Credit Transfers (CTs)?
Yes, you can. If you hold units that are equivalent to the current version within your qualification, we can apply Credit Transfers to recognise that completed learning.
Tip: Have your previous Statement of Attainment or qualification ready when you enquire, as it speeds things up.
